Our Mission

FoodShare is a community-driven initiative dedicated to solving two critical problems simultaneously: food waste and hunger. We believe that no edible food should go to waste while people in our communities go hungry.

Our platform creates a seamless connection between food donors (restaurants, hotels, caterers, and individuals) and recipients (NGOs, shelters, orphanages, and communities in need). Through technology and community partnerships, we ensure surplus food reaches those who need it most.

Our Story

FoodShare began when our founders witnessed the alarming contrast between food waste in the hospitality industry and the growing number of food-insecure individuals in our cities. What started as a small volunteer group collecting surplus food from local restaurants has evolved into a technology-powered platform serving multiple communities.

Today, we work with hundreds of food businesses and community organizations, facilitated by a network of dedicated volunteers and delivery partners. Our impact continues to grow as we expand to new regions and develop innovative solutions to food redistribution challenges.

Our Values

Compassion: We approach our work with empathy and understanding for both donors and recipients.

Efficiency: We've developed systems that ensure food reaches those in need quickly and safely.

Sustainability: By reducing food waste, we contribute to environmental conservation efforts.

Community: We believe in the power of collective action to create meaningful change.

Transparency: We maintain open communication with all our stakeholders about our operations and impact.
