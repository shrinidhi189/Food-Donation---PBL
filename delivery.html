Partner With Us

Our delivery partners play a crucial role in our mission to reduce food waste and fight hunger. They ensure that surplus food reaches those in need quickly and safely.

We work with a variety of delivery services, from established companies to local volunteer networks, to create an efficient and reliable food redistribution system.

Benefits for Partners

Social Impact

Contribute directly to fighting hunger and reducing food waste in your community

Business Growth

Expand your service offerings and reach new customer segments

Community Engagement

Build stronger relationships with local communities and organizations

Recognition

Gain visibility as a socially responsible business in your area

Become a Delivery Partner

Interested in joining our network of delivery partners? We're always looking to expand our reach and improve our service. Whether you're an established delivery company or a community volunteer group, we'd love to hear from you.

As a delivery partner, you'll receive:

  • Training on safe food handling and transportation
  • Access to our partner portal for managing deliveries
  • Recognition on our platform and marketing materials
  • Opportunities for community engagement and networking
  • Flexible scheduling to fit your operational needs
