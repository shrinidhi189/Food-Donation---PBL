How It Works

Simple steps to make a difference

Our Process

1

List Your Food

Donors easily list surplus food with details and pickup information

2

Get Matched

Our system matches donations with nearby recipients in need

3

Arrange Delivery

Coordinate pickup or delivery through our partner network

4

Make an Impact

Food reaches those in need, reducing waste and fighting hunger

For Food Donors

If you have surplus food to donate, the process is simple:

  1. Create an account and verify your food business or individual donor status
  2. List available food with details (type, quantity, preparation time, expiry)
  3. Set pickup availability windows
  4. Receive match notifications with nearby recipients
  5. Coordinate pickup with our delivery partners or volunteers
  6. Track your impact through our dashboard

For Food Recipients

If you represent an organization that needs food donations:

  1. Register your organization and complete verification
  2. Set your food needs and capacity
  3. Receive notifications of available donations in your area
  4. Accept donations that match your requirements
  5. Coordinate delivery or pickup
  6. Provide feedback on received donations

Who Can Participate

Hotels & Restaurants

Donate surplus prepared food from events, buffets, or daily operations

Individuals

Share excess home-cooked meals or unused groceries

NGOs & Organizations

Receive food donations for community programs and services

Shelters & Orphanages

Access nutritious meals for residents and program participants

Food Safety

We prioritize food safety in all our operations. All donations must meet our safety guidelines:

  • Food must be prepared and handled following proper hygiene practices
  • Perishable items must be properly refrigerated until pickup
  • All food must be within safe consumption timelines
  • Clear labeling of ingredients for allergen awareness
  • Proper packaging for safe transport

Our delivery partners are trained in safe food handling during transport.
